Warm-up with Kubernetes PodCast

I’m really trying to come up with some useful for me and for others. I’m working on an Ansible post but before that I will remove the rust and warm-up.

I’ve been working with Kubernetes for the past 2-3 years and one thing that I like about it is that you can find a lot of resources on the web. The documentation is good and you can even help with the translation to other languages besides English. Check this link.

There is one resource that I enjoy a lot to hear and that is the Kubernetes Pod Cast. Both Craig and Adam are funny and I love how the podcast is organized. Some intro, some news on the comunity, tools, and then an interview. You can always learn some nice from the podcat.

So, this is my recommendation.
